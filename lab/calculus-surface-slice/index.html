3D Surface Slice for Partial Derivatives

Surface: z = f(x,y) = sin(x)·cos(y). The translucent plane is the slice y = y₀. The thick dark curve is the intersection (a “slice” curve), and the blue line is a secant line between two points (x, y₀, f(x,y₀)) and (x+h, y₀, f(x+h,y₀)).
