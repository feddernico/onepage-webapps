Voice Refiner

Record, transcribe, and polish spoken notes locally in the browser.

Speech → draft → refined text

Turn a rough voice message into something clean, concise, and ready to send.

The app captures a spoken draft with the browser speech API, then asks WebLLM to rewrite it in your chosen tone. Use the lite model for quick cleanup or the deep model for stronger edits.

Fast passLightweight rewrite for quick notes, reminders, and short replies.
Deep passHeavier model for emails, summaries, and more nuanced restructuring.

Notes

  • Speech recognition quality depends on the browser engine and language pack.
  • The refinement step runs fully in-browser through WebLLM once the model is downloaded.
  • Deep models can take several gigabytes of GPU memory; keep the lite option as the default path.

Keyboard shortcuts: R to record or stop, Cmd/Ctrl + Enter to refine, Esc to stop recording.
